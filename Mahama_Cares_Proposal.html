EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

This proposal outlines the development of a comprehensive digital platform for the MahamaCares Fund, designed to revolutionize healthcare funding access for Ghanaian citizens. The solution encompasses a public-facing application portal, administrative management system, and integrated grant management platform, all built to the highest standards of transparency, security, and accessibility.

Strategic Value Proposition: This platform will establish Ghana as a continental leader in digital healthcare funding, providing transparent, efficient access to medical assistance while ensuring complete accountability to citizens and funding partners. The integrated grant management system will enable sophisticated tracking of funding sources from Government allocations to international donor contributions.

SCOPE OF WORK

1. PUBLIC-FACING DIGITAL PLATFORM

  • Multi-Language Public Website: English, Twi, and Ga language support with accessibility compliance
  • Real-Time Public Dashboard: Live transparency metrics showing applications processed and funds disbursed
  • Citizen Application Portal: Secure registration and application system with Ghana Card integration
  • Document Management System: Secure upload and management of medical documentation

2. ADMINISTRATIVE MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

  • Intelligent Case Management: Priority-based application queuing with automated processing
  • Multi-Role Review System: Dedicated interfaces for administrators, medical reviewers, and finance officers
  • Advanced Workflow Engine: Configurable approval workflows with audit trails
  • Medical Review Tools: Clinical assessment interface for treatment validation

3. INTEGRATED GRANT MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

  • Multi-Donor Tracking: Management of funding from Government, World Bank, UN agencies, and private foundations
  • Grant Administration: Digital storage and tracking of donor agreements and compliance requirements
  • Automated Reporting: Donor-specific reports meeting international standards
  • Financial Controls: Multi-level authorization and comprehensive audit trails

4. PAYMENT & FINANCIAL SYSTEM

  • Multi-Channel Processing: Integration with banks, mobile money, and government payment systems
  • Payment Reconciliation: Automated reconciliation with variance reporting
  • Beneficiary Portal: Secure payment tracking for beneficiaries

TECHNICAL ARCHITECTURE

Technology Stack

  • Frontend: ASP.NET Core MVC 8.0, Bootstrap 5, JavaScript/jQuery
  • Backend: ASP.NET Core 8.0, Entity Framework Core, Identity Framework
  • Database: Microsoft SQL Server with automated backups
  • Security: AES-256 encryption, TLS 1.3, multi-factor authentication
  • Infrastructure: NITA Government Data Center hosting

Compliance & Security

  • Data Protection Act 2012: Full compliance with consent management and data subject rights
  • Government Standards: NITA security requirements and government IT policies
  • Audit & Monitoring: Comprehensive audit trails and real-time monitoring
