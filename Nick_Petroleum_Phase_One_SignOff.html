What We've Understood From Our Discussions

üè™ Operations & Station Management (14 Stations)

  • Purchase Orders: Multi-level approval workflow with Operations Manager approval required before procurement
  • Fuel Deliveries: Tracking from depot to station with driver, vehicle, and transporter details; variance management between ordered and received quantities
  • Station Operations: Session-based operations with shift management, pump readings, tank dipping, and reconciliation
  • Cash Management: Daily reconciliation of fuel sales, mart sales, expenses, and banking with variance tracking
  • Real-time Visibility: Operations Manager needs live visibility across all 14 stations with alerts for variances and issues

üí∞ Finance & Accounting Integration

  • Sage Evolution as Financial Hub: All financial data must flow into Sage Evolution for reporting and compliance
  • Chart of Accounts: Station-specific account codes (e.g., 1200/001 for Station 1 Fuel Sales) with automated segmentation
  • Batch Synchronization: End-of-day summaries synced to Sage as Cashbook Batches and Journal Entries, with review before posting
  • Expense Management: Requisition and approval workflow with proper GL account mapping
  • Financial Controls: Once posted in Sage, records lock in the Mini ERP to prevent discrepancies

üë• User Management & Access Control
