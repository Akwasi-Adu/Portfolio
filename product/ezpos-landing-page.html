The Problem

Your current POS is
costing you money.

Spreadsheets, paper receipts, and disconnected systems are silently draining your business. Sound familiar?

📡

Internet goes down, sales stop

Most POS systems die when the Wi-Fi drops. In Ghana, that means hours of lost revenue and frustrated customers waiting to pay.

👟

Size 42? Let me check the back

Without variant tracking, you're guessing which sizes, colours, and styles you actually have. Customers leave while you rummage through boxes.

🏪

Branch 2 has no idea what Branch 1 sold

Multiple locations running separate systems means blind spots on stock levels, sales performance, and customer behaviour across your business.

📱

MoMo? We don't track that

Mobile money payments scattered across personal phones with no reconciliation. End-of-day cash counts never balance because half the payments are invisible.

📊

What's my profit? I'll tell you in January

No real-time visibility into margins, top sellers, dead stock, or customer credit exposure. Decisions made by gut feel, not data.

🔒

Cashier discounts, no questions asked

No approval workflows, no audit trails, no fraud detection. Unauthorised discounts, voids, and returns leak money you'll never trace.

Mobile Money Native
