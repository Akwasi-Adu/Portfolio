EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

We are pleased to present this proposal for the development of a comprehensive Mini ERP + Accounting System tailored specifically for your company. This solution is designed to replace your current Excel-based operations with a modern, integrated web application that will streamline your job costing, accounting, inventory management, and payroll processes.

The Challenge: Managing engineering and manufacturing operations through spreadsheets creates significant inefficiencies including:
  • No real-time visibility into job profitability or work-in-progress (WIP) status
  • Error-prone manual processes for invoicing, payments, and bank reconciliation
  • Inability to properly track multi-currency transactions (critical for imported materials)
  • No audit trail for financial transactions
  • Time-consuming month-end closing processes
  • Disconnected data between sales, operations, inventory, and accounting
Our Solution: A purpose-built Mini ERP + Accounting web application with integrated modules for:
  • Job Costing (Primary Focus) - Quote to job conversion, WIP tracking, progress billing, job profitability analysis
  • General Ledger - Chart of accounts, journal entries, period close, financial statements
  • Accounts Receivable - Customer management, invoicing, receipts, withholding tax handling
  • Accounts Payable - Supplier management, purchase orders, bills, payments
  • Inventory Management - Items, stock tracking, weighted average/FIFO valuation
  • Basic Payroll - Ghana-compliant PAYE and SSNIT calculations
  • Multi-Currency & Banking - Foreign currency transactions, revaluation, bank reconciliation
